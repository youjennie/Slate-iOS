# Slate — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: June 22, 2026_

Slate ("the app", "we") is a personal photo journal that helps you capture daily
moments and see your progress over time. We care about your privacy and keep data
collection to the minimum required for the app to work. This policy explains what
we handle and how.

## What we collect

- **Your photos and journal entries.** Photos you capture or import, along with the
  date and the space/category you assign, are stored **locally on your device**.
- **Your name.** The name you enter during onboarding (or that Apple provides at
  sign-in) is stored locally and shown in the app.
- **Sign in with Apple identifier.** When you sign in with Apple, we store the stable
  user identifier Apple provides so we can keep you signed in and detect if you revoke
  access. We do **not** receive or store your real Apple ID password. If you use
  Apple's "Hide My Email," we only ever see the relay address.

We do **not** use third‑party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

## How your data is used

- To display your journal, calendar, streaks, and progress.
- To keep you signed in and to sign you out automatically if you revoke Apple access.
- To send you the optional daily reminder notification (only if you enable it).

## Photo sharing & privacy control

By default your photos are **private and stay on your device**. The app includes a
**Photo Privacy** setting (on by default). Your photos are only eligible to appear in
the social feed if you explicitly turn Photo Privacy **off**. You can change this at
any time in **Settings ▸ Photo Privacy**.

## Optional features that use third parties

These features are **off unless you enable them**, and the app works fully without them:

- **AI "future self" image (optional).** If you enable this feature, the photo you choose
  is sent to **Google's Gemini API** to generate an image. The image is processed to
  produce the result and is subject to
  [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy). We do not send this
  data anywhere else.
- **Social feed / cloud sync (optional, future).** If a future version connects to a
  backend (e.g., Firebase), only content you choose to share — and never private photos
  — would be uploaded. This policy will be updated before any such feature is enabled.

## Notifications

If you enable reminders, the app schedules **local notifications** on your device. No
notification content is sent to a server.

## Data storage & retention

- Your photos, entries, profile name, and settings are stored **on your device** using
  Apple's on-device storage (SwiftData / UserDefaults).
- Deleted items are kept in a recoverable state for up to **30 days**, then permanently
  removed.
- We do not maintain server-side copies of your private content.

## Your choices & rights

- **Delete everything.** Use **Settings ▸ Delete Account** to permanently erase all your
  photos, spaces, profile, and on-device data.
- **Turn off optional features** (AI, notifications, photo sharing) at any time in Settings.
- **Revoke Apple access** from your device's Settings ▸ your Apple ID ▸ Sign in with Apple.

## Children

Slate is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from
children under 13.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be reflected here
with a new "Last updated" date.
